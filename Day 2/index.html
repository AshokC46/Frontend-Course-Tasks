About the Department

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Nbkr institute of science and technology is a dynamic academic unit dedicated to providing high-quality education and fostering cutting-edge research in the field. We offer a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.Tech. in Electrical and Electronics Engineering, M.Tech. in Power Systems, and M.Tech. in Control Systems, among others. Our highly qualified faculty are actively engaged in research in diverse areas such as Power Systems, Control Systems, Power Electronics, Renewable Energy, and Signal Processing. Equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories, the department provides students with ample opportunities for hands-on learning and research. We encourage student involvement through clubs like the IEEE Student Branch and organize various workshops, seminars, and competitions. Our graduates are highly sought after by leading companies in the industry, ensuring successful career paths.
